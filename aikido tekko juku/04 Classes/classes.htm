Schedule:
 
   
  • The dojo will be closed during national holidays and snow emergencies.

Fee Schedule

One time registration fee: $20
Full time: $80
Weekend Only: $40
Daily rate: $10

  • Fees are payable (cash or check) at the beginning of each month.
  • Married couples and committed domestic partners living at the same address can practice for one at full price and the second at half price.
  • Members starting after the middle of the month may pro-rate their second-month dues.
  • All Aikido Tekkojuku Boston students must register and sign a waiver before they practice.
  • Visitors who are full time students at other aikido dojos can practice 3 times each year without fee.
