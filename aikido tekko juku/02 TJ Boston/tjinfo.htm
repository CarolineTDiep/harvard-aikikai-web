About Us:
 
 
   

Tekkojuku is a special place. To understand why, it is first important to understand what Tekkojuku is and is not.

It is not a commercial business.

No one here gets paid. Everyone is a volunteer, dedicated to learning and improving their aikido. Unlike commercial schools, there are no contracts, no commitments, and no hard sell. We are here to learn – we invite you to learn with us.

It is not a Fight Club.

Aikido is an effective means of self defense and a deadly fighting art. It is also, however, much more. Aikido is the only martial art which does not include an “opponent” and does not include competition. It is the only martial art which focuses on controlling the attacker without hurting him or her. Students and teachers of all ages can practice together safely and at their own pace.

It is not a meditation center.

Aikido, to yield its benefits, must retain its character as a martial art, not a meditation art. The benefits of study emerge from dedicated practice in a martially serious way. While this is done in a safe, non-competitive manner, physical application of the techniques on the mat is critical to understanding the art.

Given what Tekkojuku is not, what is it?

It is a dojo.

Dojo literally means place of the way, and it is the place where we seriously study the martial way of aikido. Serious does not mean stern, and it does not mean not having fun. It does mean focused.

It is fun.

One of the primary benefits of aikido is that it is fun to practice. As a non-commercial dojo, Tekkojuku is focused on the members' experiences, and as a member run institution the focus of the dojo is on making the study of aikido as rewarding as possible.

It is dedicated to teaching aikido.

Tekkojuku has among the most highly ranked teachers in New England, with multiple teachers at or above third dan (third degree black belt). Moreover, in addition to being very skilled, the instructors are focused on – and enjoy – teaching. As volunteers, their reward is helping students improve.

Because of all of these facts, Tekkojuku is a unique and exciting place to learn aikido. Thank you for your interest. We look forward to practicing with you.
