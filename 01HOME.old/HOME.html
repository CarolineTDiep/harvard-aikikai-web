Harvard Aikikai is an undergraduate club dedicated to teaching members of the Harvard community (students, faculty, staff, and alumni) about the principles and techniques of Aikido.

All are welcome, and we encourage all who are interested to contact our president for more information or to visit a class.

Harvard Aikikai is affiliated with the United States Aikido Federation.
