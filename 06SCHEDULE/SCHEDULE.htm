Class Schedule

Our weekly practices are:

  • Mondays 6:30-8:25 pm
  • Thursdays 8:30-10:00 pm
  • Fridays 6-8pm New England Aikikai (ask other members about this if you're interested)
  • Saturdays 1-3 pm (two 1h practices)

Monday and Thursday classes take place in the Malkin Athletic Center, Fitness Room 2. Saturday classes take place in the Quadrangle Recreational Athletic Center, Racquetball Court 2 (downstairs by the locker rooms). For finding the two buildings, this map is helpful. Friday classes take place at New England Aikikai (NEA). E-mail us with questions at harvardaikikai-at-gmail-dot-com.

Classes are run through the normal academic schedule, and we are off during school holidays when the Quadrangle Recreational Athletic Center is closed.
