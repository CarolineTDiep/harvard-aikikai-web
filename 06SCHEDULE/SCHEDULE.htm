Class Schedule

Classes are run through the normal academic schedule, and we are off during school holidays when the Maklin Athletic Center or the Quadrangle Recreational Athletic Center is closed. We ask that everyone be a few minutes early to set up the mats and stay a few minutes after to help put them away.

All classes take place in either the Maklin Athletic Center or the Quadrangle Recreational Athletic Center or at New England Aikikai (NEA). E-mail us with questions at harvardaikikai-at-gmail-dot-com.
