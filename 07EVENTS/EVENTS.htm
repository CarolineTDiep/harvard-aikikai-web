Upcoming Events Schedule
  

 

We're having regular practice until the end of the year, and will be reserving space for the fall semester in mid-september, with practice resuming soon after that.

Each year, we hold annual field trips to the Cornell Aikikai and other seminars, and participate in Arts First at Harvard. We also hold seminar parties and end-of-year get-togethers.
