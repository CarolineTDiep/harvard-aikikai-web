Fall 2014 Kickoff Events

  • Student Activities Fair, Friday Sept 5th, 4-7pm

    We are going to be at the Student Activities Fair to show some cool Aikido videos and answer any questions you might have about Aikido and our club.

    Drop by table 377 to learn more about Aikido and the club while enjoying yummy food.

  • Get Involved Weekend! @ Sever 111, Saturday Sept 6th, 12-1pm

    Dolita Cathcart Sensei, our senior instructor is giving a presentation on Aikido philosophy and history. There will be a Q&A session afterwards.
