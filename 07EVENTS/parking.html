To buy a visitor parking pass ($5, must be purchased and printed in advance):
  1. Go to the Harvard visitor permit website. The last step fails in Firefox, but works in Chrome.
  2. Select "Visitor" at the bottom of the first screen
  3. Click the button to register (if you have not already).
  4. Choose "Visitor to campus" for Department and enter 7700 as the Department Code.
  5. Under "Select a parking lot to begin", select "Observatory Lot weekends" (it's closest to the QRAC).
  6. Select "Event Participant" for Affiliation, and specify "Harvard Aikikai" or similar.
  7. Enter the date and your plate number and state and click "Add parking permits"
  8. Click Checkout, then I Agree, then Complete Order.
  9. Once you've paid through PayPal, click "Return to Harvard Parking Services". After waiting a moment for the redirect, you'll get the results screen. Click the PDF link to download it. Print it and bring it with you!
The Observatory lot is located at 60 Concord Street, a short walk from the Harvard QRAC (Google Map)
