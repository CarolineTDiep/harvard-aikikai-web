2005-2006 Normal Class Schedule
   
Classes are run through the normal academic schedule, and we are off during school holidays when the Maklin Athletic Center or the Quadrangle Recreational Athletic Center is closed. We ask that everyone be 15 minutes early to set up the mats and stay a few minutes after to help put them away.
  
   
Monday (QRAC) 6:30 p.m. - 8:00 p.m.
Wednesday (MAC) 7:00 p.m. - 8:30 p.m.
Thursday (QRAC) 7:00 p.m. - 8:30 p.m.
Friday (MAC) 7:30 p.m. - 9:00 p.m.
Saturday (MAC) 10:00 a.m. - 12:00 p.m.
 
   
All classes take place in either in the mezzanine of the Malkin Athletic Center (MAC) or the Quadrangle Recreational Athletic Center (QRAC).
