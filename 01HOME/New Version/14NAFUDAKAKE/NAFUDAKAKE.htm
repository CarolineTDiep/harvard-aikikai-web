Harvard Aikikai Nafudakake:
   

A Nafudakake is an essential part of a Japanese Dojo, listing the members of their dojo next to their respective rank. Our Nafudakake lists O-Sensei Morihei Ueshiba, the present Doshu Moriteru Ueshiba, and honors the late Mitsunari Kanai-Shihan before starting with our club membership.

 
   
 
   

     As yudansha tests are few and far between and are taken at the regional seminars, we have not listed the requirements for the dan rankings.
